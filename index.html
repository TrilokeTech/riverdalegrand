About The Project

Welcome to Riverdale Grand

Riverdale Grand is more than a residential address, it’s a thoughtfully envisioned riverside community created for families seeking space, comfort, and timeless elegance. Situated on the New Kharadi-Shivane Riverside Road, this landmark development combines modern architecture with lush open spaces, creating an environment where every day feels extraordinary.

Designed as the flagship luxury offering within Duville’s Riverdale Township, Riverdale Grand delivers spacious residences, uninterrupted skyline views, and a lifestyle inspired by nature without compromising urban convenience.
